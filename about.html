Nick Hummel and Tommy Quiros have been playing and writing music together for 9 years.
We Should Leave This Tree's upcoming first album is a collection of their best original material written
between the ages of 16 to 21. The band's debut self-titled record - out September 2nd - has been well over a year
in the production process. The present-day WSLTT lineup of Nick Hummel, Tommy Quiros, Drew Anderson, & Ben Minahan, has undertaken
the production of this first full-length We Should Leave This Tree record completely independently.
